Welcome to the simple Maze!

The goal of this game is to turn on the button then go to the exit with at least 1 health. Getting touched by enemies and traps will lead to a hp loss.
Attacks can kill the nearby enemies.

Game controls:

W: moves up
A: moves left
S: moves down
D: moves right
J: attack(if possible)
Space: center the camera on the player
Y: Lock/unlock camera center to the player center(always centering)
F1: zoom in
F2: zoom out
F3: set the camera center to map center
Esc: go to menu screen

Up ↑: camera moves up
Down ↓: camera moves up
Left ←：camera moves up
Right →: camera moves up

Cheating Code:tum
